# Jiff — Pitch Write-Up
## Seed Round · ₹1.5 Crore (~$180K) · March 2026

---

## The one-line pitch

**Jiff turns "I don't know what to cook" into five full recipes in moments — for any fridge, any diet, any language.**

---

## The problem

People make 3–5 food decisions every day. That is over 1,000 decisions a year, made with zero inspiration, ending in frustration, food waste, or unnecessary delivery. "What should I cook tonight?" is asked 3.4 billion times daily — and nobody has truly solved it.

Recipe apps give you rabbit holes, not answers. Meal kits require subscriptions and scheduled deliveries. ChatGPT gives generic suggestions with no memory of your preferences. Delivery apps solve the symptom, not the cause.

The real problem: people have food in their fridge but no idea what to make with it. Jiff solves this at the moment of decision.

---

## The solution

Open Jiff. Type what is in your fridge. Get five full recipes — ingredients, steps, nutrition, step timers — in less than a minute.

Jiff is not a recipe discovery app. It is a thinking partner that knows your cuisine preferences, dietary requirements, family members' needs, and health goals. Every suggestion is personalised, not generic.

**What makes Jiff different from everything else:**

- **Fridge-first** — recipes from what you already have, not what you need to buy
- **Genuinely personalised** — remembers your food type, spice level, 28 cuisine preferences, family dietary needs
- **India-built** — ₹99/mo pricing, 7 Indian languages (Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Bengali, Malayalam), 28 cuisines including Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Rajasthani, Bengali and more
- **Full journey** — not just recipes. Week Planner, Goal Plans (high protein, weight loss, diabetic), Kids Meals, grocery delivery via Blinkit/Zepto, WhatsApp bot, Insights dashboard
- **Real AI, not a wrapper** — custom prompt engineering, structured JSON parsing, family mode that adjusts recipes per-member, ingredient translator from regional names to English
